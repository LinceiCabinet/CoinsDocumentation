About the Archive

Lincei Cabinet is an independent digital archive dedicated to providing English translations of Italian numismatic literature.

The project focuses on primary sources, early catalogues, and regional studies that remain inaccessible to non-Italian readers.

This archive is non-commercial and intended solely for research, reference, and educational use.
